## ✨ WHAT'S NEW

### 1. 🎯 Health Goals Tracker
Set and track personalized health goals across 8 categories with visual progress tracking, deadlines, and success rate monitoring.

**Key Features**:
- Create unlimited health goals
- 8 categories (Cycle, Symptoms, Fitness, Nutrition, Sleep, Weight, Mental Health, Other)
- Progress bars and completion tracking
- Priority levels and deadlines
- Goal statistics dashboard

---

### 2. 🏆 Health Challenges & Achievements
Gamified health tracking with XP, levels, challenges, and achievements to keep you motivated!

**Key Features**:
- XP and leveling system (Level 1-∞)
- 8 pre-defined challenges
- 10 achievements to unlock
- Active challenge tracking (max 3)
- Streak tracking
- Difficulty levels (easy, medium, hard)

**Challenges Include**:
- Daily Logger, Water Warrior, Cycle Master
- Exercise Enthusiast, Mood Master, Sleep Champion
- Wellness Warrior, Health Hero

---

### 3. 🎤 Voice Journal
Speak your thoughts and let AI capture them! Voice-powered journaling with automatic mood detection.

**Key Features**:
- Voice-to-text recording
- Real-time speech transcription
- Automatic mood detection (7 moods)
- Date-based organization
- Mood visualization with emojis
- Browser speech recognition support

---

### 4. 📸 Photo Symptom Tracker
Track skin conditions and symptoms visually with photos. Perfect for acne, skin conditions, and more!

**Key Features**:
- Photo upload for 6 symptom categories
- Severity tracking (mild, moderate, severe)
- 12 body location options
- Progress analysis (improving/worsening/stable)
- Before/after comparisons
- Full-screen photo viewer
- Privacy-first (local storage)
